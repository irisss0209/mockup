Frequently Asked Questions

APU FitNutri is a student organization dedicated to promoting fitness and nutrition education at our university. We provide resources, activities, and support to help students lead healthier lifestyles.

Joining APU FitNutri is simple! Just fill out the registration form on our website to become a member. There are no membership fees required.

APU FitNutri offers a variety of fitness activities, including group workouts, fitness challenges, yoga sessions, and more.

Yes! APU FitNutri provides nutrition resources, including healthy eating tips, portion controls, and recipes on nutrition-related topics.
