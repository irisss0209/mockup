Introduction


Welcome to APU FitNutri Portion Control Hub!
Whether you're a seasoned member or new to our community, we're here to guide you on the path to balanced eating.

In the midst of student life, portion control often gets overlooked. But fear not! We're here to show you how mastering portion sizes can fuel your success and vitality.

Within our hub, you'll find practical tips, engaging activities, and resources to help you navigate portion sizes with confidence. Whether you want to manage your weight, boost energy, or simply have a healthier relationship with food, mastering portion control is key.

Join us on this journey towards healthier eating habits. Ready to take control of your plate?
Let's dive in together – because every bite counts!

Protein:
Essential for muscle repair, immune function, and overall body maintenance. Examples include tofu, chicken, fish, eggs, and soybeans.
Vegetables:
Packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber, promoting overall health and disease prevention. Examples include carrots, cherries, eggplant, fruits, kiwi, and mango.
Carbohydrates:
Primary energy source for the body, fueling daily activities and exercise. Examples include rice, potatoes, tomatoes, and chips.
Fats:
Important for hormone production, nutrient absorption, and cell function. Healthy sources include avocado, seeds, peanuts, and certain types of pizza.
