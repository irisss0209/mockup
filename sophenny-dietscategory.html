Short Intro On Each Diet Category

What is Mediterranean Diet?

Based on the traditional eating patterns of countries bordering the Mediterranean Sea, this diet emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts, seeds, olive oil, and moderate amounts of fish, poultry, and dairy. It limits red meat and processed foods.

What is Plant-Based Diet?

A plant-based diet is a dietary approach that primarily focuses on foods derived from plants, such as fruits, vegetables, grains, nuts, seeds, and legumes, while minimizing or excluding animal products. The emphasis is on consuming whole, minimally processed foods that come from plants, with the goal of improving health, reducing environmental impact, and promoting ethical considerations related to animal welfare.

What is Flexitarian Diet?

A flexible approach to eating that emphasizes plant-based foods while allowing occasional consumption of meat and other animal products. Flexitarians primarily focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts, seeds, and plant-based protein sources.

What is Intermittent Fasting?

Intermittent fasting is an eating pattern that involves cycling between periods of eating and fasting. It doesn't prescribe specific foods to eat but rather focuses on when to eat them. Popular methods include the 16/8 method, 5:2 diet, and alternate-day fasting. Intermittent fasting may offer benefits such as weight loss, improved metabolic health, and better blood sugar control. However, it's essential to approach it with caution and consider individual health needs.

What is Paleo Diet?

Inspired by the presumed diet of Paleolithic humans, this diet focuses on consuming foods that would have been available to our ancestors, such as lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds. It excludes processed foods, grains, legumes, and dairy.

What is Low-Fat Diet?

Focuses on reducing the intake of fats, particularly saturated and trans fats, in favor of carbohydrates and sometimes protein. It may be recommended for individuals seeking to lower cholesterol levels or manage weight.
